George Orwell

(Eric Arthur Blair)

"In a time of universal deceit - telling the truth is a revolutionary act."

Orwell's press card portrait, 1943.

Here is a short timeline of Orwell's life:

  • 1903 - Born in Motihari, India
  • 1914 - First work published, a poem titled Awake Young Men of England
  • 1928 - Moved to Paris
  • 1933 - First novel, Down and Out in Paris, published under pseudonym George Orwell
  • 1945 - Animal Farm published
  • 1950 - Died in University College Hospital
